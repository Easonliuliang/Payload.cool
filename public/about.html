About Us

Payload.cool is built by developers, for developers.

Our Mission

We believe that developer tools should be fast, private, and easy to use. In an era where data privacy is paramount, we saw a need for a JSON formatter that doesn't require sending sensitive data to a remote server.

Why We Built This

Many online JSON tools are outdated, slow, or riddled with invasive ads and tracking. We wanted to create a modern, "cool" alternative that respects your privacy and enhances your workflow with features like Graph View and Schema Validation.

The "Cool" Series

Payload.cool is the first in a planned series of privacy-first developer utilities. Stay tuned for more!
