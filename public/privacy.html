Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 19, 2025

Core Privacy Promise: Data is processed locally, no upload.
Payload.cool operates entirely within your web browser. We do not transmit, store, or view the JSON data you paste into the tool.

1. Data Processing

All parsing, formatting, and validation logic runs client-side using JavaScript (Web Workers). Your data never leaves your device.

2. Information Collection

We do not collect personal information. We may use anonymous analytics (like local storage for preferences) to improve user experience, but no personally identifiable information (PII) is tracked.

3. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for hosting (e.g., Vercel) or analytics (e.g., Google Analytics) which may collect standard usage data (IP address, browser type) subject to their own privacy policies.

4. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the service implies acceptance.

5. Contact

For questions, please contact us via our GitHub repository.
